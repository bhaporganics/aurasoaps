Crafted with Devotion

Each bar is handmade following ancient Ayurvedic principles with modern care.

1

Ingredient Selection

Only the finest organic rose petals are dried gently and ground into micro-fine powder to retain their natural color, fragrance, and skin-loving benefits.

2

Cold Process Method

Base oils (coconut oil, olive oil, and shea butter) are blended at low temperature with the rose petal extract powder that is handmade using the authentic cold-process technique to preserve nutrients and ensure a luxurious, creamy lather.

3

Natural Curing

Each bar is air-dried for 6 weeks, allowing natural saponification, improved hardness, and the development of a gentle, long-lasting bar.

4

Packaging

The Rose petal soap is ready to be packed with clean hands adding more divinity in look. Together with these, the soap is ready to clean you by adding more fragrance to your body
