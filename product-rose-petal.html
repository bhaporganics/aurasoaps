Crafted with Devotion

Each bar is handmade following ancient Ayurvedic principles with modern care.

1

Ingredient Selection

Only the finest organic rose petals are dried gently and ground into micro-fine powder to retain their natural color, fragrance, and skin-loving benefits.

2

Cold Process Method

Base oils (coconut oil, olive oil, and shea butter) are blended at low temperatures using the authentic cold-process technique to preserve nutrients and ensure a luxurious, creamy lather.

3

Natural Curing

Each bar is air-dried for 6 weeks, allowing natural saponification, improved hardness, and the development of a gentle, long-lasting bar.

4

Rose Infusion Blend

At trace, the rose petal powder is folded in slowly to maintain its natural aroma and deliver mild exfoliation without irritation—giving the soap its signature rosy glow.
